The Mercedes-Benz C-Class is the brand's compact executive car, positioned between the entry-level A-Class and the larger E-Class. Known for its blend of luxury, technology, and sporty driving dynamics, the C-Class is one of Mercedes-Benz's most popular and versatile models, available in sedan, coupe, convertible, and wagon body styles.
